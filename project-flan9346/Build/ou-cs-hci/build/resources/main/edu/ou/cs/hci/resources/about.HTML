FridgTrackr

Version 9.1

FridgTrackr is an application designed to help users track the contents of their fridge. After they go grocery shopping they can enter what they bought. After they have they can browse the database of recipes to see what they can make with what they have. If the user doesn't not have the required ingredients the app will inform them, and allow them to add these items to their grocery list (another database our app manages).
